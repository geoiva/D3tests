Site for standalone D3 experiments, tests and examples:

Measuring Distance & Azimuth:
Explains how a Plate-Carré projection can serve as a nifty tool to show bearing and distance from a location on Earth., where you would normally use a equidistant projection....
Tool to explore tracks withs stops:
Shows stops and distance travelled in different ways -- as map, as time and distance equally scaled, as time scaled dependent of distance and as distance scaled dependent of time... Examples shown are a GPS walk and Napoleons Moscow campaign.
D3 and Javascript data filter test:
Explains difference between JavaScript standard Array.filter() and d3.filter() function...
Distance Perception:
Gepercipieerde afstanden in Europa in 1977-1981 en 2013 -- Studenten situeren Oost-Europese steden te ver weg en Zuid-Europese te dichtbij.
